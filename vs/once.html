Filmsera vs Once

Once is built for frictionless event guest photos. Filmsera is built for groups who want a deeper Look shelf and a shared Reveal ritual — and who are fine installing the app.

Straight talk. Once is stronger when guests should join without downloading anything. Filmsera does not offer no-download guests. Everyone needs Filmsera on iPhone.

When to pick which

Filmsera: Choose Filmsera when your group already lives on iPhone, cares about which Look they’re shooting through, and wants a timed Reveal plus Recap afterward.

Once: Choose Once when guest friction is the #1 problem — e.g. a wedding where many people will not install another app.

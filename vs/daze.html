Filmsera vs Daze

Daze brings vintage texture for individual shooting. Filmsera turns the roll into a shared group ritual — Host, Guests, Reveal.

Straight talk. Daze is about personal texture and camera feel. Filmsera is about the group Film. Different jobs; both can be great.

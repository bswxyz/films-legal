Filmsera vs POV

POV is strong when guests need to join an event camera quickly. Filmsera trades that frictionlessness for a live Look shelf and a multiplayer Film ritual.

Straight talk. POV wins on no-download / low-friction guest join for events. Filmsera requires the iPhone app for Hosts and Guests. We don’t pretend otherwise.

When to pick which

Filmsera: Choose Filmsera when the shelf matters — Disposable tonight, VHS tomorrow — and everyone can install the app.

POV: Choose POV when you need guests in with almost no steps, and a deep vintage Look library is secondary.

Films is a shared disposable camera — shoot the night through every look, and nobody sees a frame until it develops.

How do shared films work?

Create a film, name it, choose a look, and set when it should develop. Share the invite link or code, and the people you invite shoot into the same film. No one sees any photo until the reveal timer hits zero — then the whole album appears for everyone at once.

Can I see photos before the film develops?

No — that's the point. Frames stay hidden while you shoot. If you host the film, you can choose to reveal it early; otherwise everyone unwraps it together when the timer ends.

What do I get with Films Pro?

The core camera and films are free. Films Pro unlocks the full set of premium camera packs, recap styles, and more — Weekly, Monthly, or Annual, with a free trial on the Weekly plan.

How do I manage my subscription?

Films Pro renews through your Apple Account. Manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my data or account?

Remove any photo or film in the app, or fully erase your account in Settings → Delete account. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, the films you host, your photos, and your membership records.

How do I stop the waitlist / launch emails?

Use the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send, or visit getfilmsera.cam/unsubscribe from the link in your email. It takes effect immediately. To delete your email address entirely, write to support@getfilmsera.cam.

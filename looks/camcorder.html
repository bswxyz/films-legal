Camcorder / DCR

Living-room documentary feel — MiniDV texture, DCR-era color, interlaced nostalgia.

Open in Filmsera

When to use it

  • Family days, reunions, and "we should film this" energy
  • Events where motion and chatter matter as much as polish
  • Anyone who misses the Handycam on the bookshelf

Texture notes

Slightly cooler video color, soft resolution, and that MiniDV documentary cast. Feels like a still pulled from a home video more than a film negative. Honest, a little clinical, very nostalgic.

Good to know.

What exactly is Filmsera?+

A group film camera for iPhone. Shoot Shots through 24 Looks, Host a shared Film Guests join, and open every Frame together at Reveal.

Can I see photos before they develop?+

No — Frames stay hidden while you shoot. When Reveal hits, the entire Film appears at once. You can Reveal early if you can't wait.

Do guests need to download the app?+

Yes. Guests need Filmsera on iPhone to join and shoot. A QR or join link opens the app (or the App Store if they don't have it yet).

What's free, and what's Filmsera Pro?+

Core camera and Films are free. Filmsera Pro unlocks premium Look packs, Recap styles, and more — Weekly, Monthly, or Annual. Prices show in-app before you subscribe.

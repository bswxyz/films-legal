# Product note — App Clip / browser guest join gap

**Status:** product roadmap flag — **do not market as available**  
**Date:** 2026-07-18

## Competitor lead

Event / disposable-camera competitors — **Once**, **POV**, **Scene**, and **Dazz** (and similar event apps) — lead guest acquisition with **no-download or App Clip join**. Hosts scan a QR; Guests shoot without a full App Store install. That friction reduction is the category’s loudest conversion hook for weddings and parties.

## What Filmsera has today

- Host creates a Film, picks a Look, shares QR / join link (`getfilmsera.cam/join/<code>`)
- **Filmsera QR still requires an iPhone app install** — Guests must download Filmsera to join and shoot Shots
- Timed Reveal, Recap, Live Activity, 24 Looks

Marketing (FAQ, `/vs/*`, Journal) states this honestly. We **never** claim no-download guests.

## Recommendation (next product priority)

1. **iOS App Clip** for join-by-QR guest capture, **or**
2. **Browser guest capture** for no-install / broader device coverage

Until one of those ships: keep positioning on Looks + shared Film + Reveal + Recap, and keep guest-install expectations explicit. **Do not market App Clip or browser join as available.**

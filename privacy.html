Films

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Films ("Films," "we," "us") is a camera app for capturing and sharing photos through film-style cameras, including shared "films" you create with other people. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data, we do not run ads, we do not use it for cross-app tracking, and we do not use your photos or content to train artificial-intelligence models.

Information we collect

AccountIf you sign in, your email address and a display name you choose. Sign-in is handled by our authentication provider, Clerk. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the basic profile (and, if you allow it, email) you approve.
Your photos & filmsThe photos (and their thumbnails) you capture and add to a film, the film's title and settings (such as the chosen look and reveal time), capture timestamps, and which member captured each frame.
MembershipThe display name you use in a shared film, and the films you create, join, or are invited to.
PurchasesIf you subscribe to Films Pro, Apple processes the payment. We receive your subscription status — not your card number or full payment details.
Basic operational dataOur backend providers process standard technical information needed to deliver the service (for example, the requests your app makes and timestamps). We do not add third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
Waitlist emailIf you joined our pre-launch waitlist on getfilmsera.cam, we store the email address you submitted, the source/page it came from, the signup date, and — if you ticked the consent box — a record of that consent. We use it only to send a small number of launch emails, and you can unsubscribe at any time. See "Waitlist & launch emails" below.

We do not collect your location, contacts, microphone, advertising identifier (IDFA), or biometric data, and we do not embed third-party analytics, advertising, or cross-app tracking.

Some of Films works entirely on your device. When you sign in and use a shared film, the photos and details for that film are synced to our backend so the people you invite can take part and the film can develop. Photos you keep only on your own device are not uploaded to us.

How we use your information

  • To run the camera and films, develop your rolls, and keep everyone in a shared film in sync.
  • To store your photos and let you revisit, save, recap, share, or delete them.
  • To attribute each frame to the member who captured it within a film.
  • To provide and improve the service, prevent abuse, and respond to support requests.
  • To process and manage your subscription (via Apple).

Who we share it with (service providers)

We share data only with the processors that operate the app, under their own terms, and only as needed to run Films:

ClerkSign-in and account management (your email and user ID).
ConvexOur backend and file storage (your display name, film details, uploaded photos and thumbnails, and timestamps).
AppleProcesses App Store subscription payments and provides sign-in with Apple.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service. If Films is ever involved in a merger or acquisition, we will require the successor to honor this policy.

People you share a film with

A shared film is visible to the people you invite to it. The photos you add, your member display name, and the frames attributed to you are shown to other members of that film. Only add people you trust, and only capture content you're comfortable sharing with the film.

Legal bases (EEA / UK — GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data on these bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the app and the features you ask for (account, films, photos, subscription).
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve Films, balanced against your rights.
  • Consent — for anything optional we ask you to opt into; you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Legal obligation — where we must process data to comply with the law.

Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data (you can edit your display name in the app).
  • Delete your data — remove any photo or film in the app, or delete your whole account in Settings.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these, email support@getfilmsera.cam. We will respond within the time required by applicable law (generally within 30 days). We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

US state privacy rights (including California)

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising — and we never have. There is nothing to opt out of because we don't do it. We don't use your data for targeted advertising or "sell" it as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) and similar US state laws.

If you are a resident of California or another US state with privacy rights, you may request to know, access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising these rights. To make a request, email support@getfilmsera.cam; you may appeal a decision by replying to our response. The categories of personal information we collect, our purposes, and the providers we disclose it to (only to operate the app) are described above and in "Who we share it with."

Your choices & deletion

  • Delete a photo or film: remove it in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account permanently removes your profile, the films you host, your photos, and your membership records.
  • Subscriptions: manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account settings.

Waitlist & launch emails

Before launch, people could join a waitlist on getfilmsera.cam by submitting an email address. We use those addresses solely to send a small number of launch-related emails, then we stop. Every marketing email includes a one-click unsubscribe link, and you can remove yourself at any time at getfilmsera.cam/unsubscribe. Unsubscribing suppresses any further emails; to delete your address entirely, email support@getfilmsera.cam. We do not sell or rent waitlist emails, and we do not share them for anyone else's advertising.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as we need it for the purposes above:

  • Account data (email, display name) — until you delete your account.
  • Photos, films & membership records — until you delete the photo or film, or delete your account.
  • Waitlist email — until shortly after launch, or until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it, whichever comes first. If you unsubscribe, we keep a minimal suppression record so we don't email you again.
  • Subscription status — for as long as needed to provide and account for your subscription.

We may retain limited records longer where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. Backups are cycled out on a rolling basis.

International transfers

Films and its providers may process and store data in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses) for international transfers.

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS) and stored with our backend provider. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and limit access to it.

Children

Films is intended for people aged 13 and older (or the higher minimum age required in your country, such as 16 in parts of the EEA). It is not directed to children under that age, and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Complaints

If you have a concern about how we handle your data, please contact us first at support@getfilmsera.cam so we can try to resolve it. If you are in the EEA or UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will revise the date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.

Contact

Films — Privacy. Questions or requests: support@getfilmsera.cam
